Chicken Tortilla Soup

Description

A twist on some chicken soup for the soul.

Ingredients

  • 3 Boneless Chicken Breasts
  • 32 oz Chicken Broth
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin
  • 1 1/2 tsp Chili Powder
  • 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 Can of Corn, drained
  • 1 Can of Black Beans, drained
  • Jar of Preferred Salsa
  • Corn Tortilla, optional

Directions

  1. Fry chicken in olive oil; set aside to cool and shred
  2. Add shredded chicken, cumin, chili powder, lemon juice, and half the jar of salsa to pan to simmer
  3. Bring chicken broth to a boil
  4. Add corn, black beans, other half of salsa, and the chicken mixture to the broth
  5. Stir every 10 minutes for 1.5 hours
  6. Add strips of corn tortilla, allow to soften, and serve
