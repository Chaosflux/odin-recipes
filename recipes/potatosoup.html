Potato Soup

Description

A simple potato soup to warm the soul.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bag of Green Onions, thinly sliced
  • 5 lb Bag of Red Potatoes, chopped
  • 1 Carton of Half and Half
  • 2 Cans of Cream of Chicken
  • 2 Cans of Cream of Mushroom
  • 32 oz Chicken Broth
  • 1 Stick of Unsalted Butter

Directions

  1. Melt butter in stock pot on medium heat
  2. Add green onions, potatoes, salt, and pepper; fry for 10 minutes
  3. Add the 4 cans of soup; stir to coat all ingredients
  4. Add half and half, broth, and water if needed to submerge ingredients; boil for 10 minutes
  5. Lower heat to simmer; stir every 10 minutes for 1.5 hours
  6. Mash potatoes lightly and serve
