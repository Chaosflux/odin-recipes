Zuppa Toscana

Description

A delicious take on Olive Garden's Zuppa Toscana soup.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Mild Italian Sausage
  • 32oz Chicken Broth
  • 2-3 lbs Russet Potatoes
  • 1 Cup Heavy Whipping Cream

Directions

  1. Slice potatoes into thin chips and set aside
  2. Brown sausage, drain and set aside
  3. Bring chicken broth to a boil
  4. Add potatoes and boil until fork tender; ~20 minutes
  5. Add sausage and heavy whipping cream; simmer ~10 minutes then serve
